About Reset Rituals

Reset Rituals was built on a simple idea. Self-care isn’t one mood. Some days you need to power through. Other days you need to shut it all down.

Every product is split into two modes. Low Power Mode for getting through it, and Do Not Disturb for shutting it down. Your routine, ready to meet you wherever you are.

No hustle culture. No wellness performance. Just rituals that fit your day.
